Instructions

  • Left-click to paint a cell with the selected layer.
    • Left-click and drag to draw a line.
  • Right-click to clear all layers on the selected cell.
    • Right-click and drag to clear all cells in the selected area.
  • Right-click without dragging away from any painted cell to change layers.
  • Point at a cell and type "=" or "+" to move the VDD contact to that cell.
  • Point at a cell and type "-" to move the GND contact to that cell.
  • Point at a cell and type "A", "B", "C", "D", or "Y" to move the chosen contact to that cell.
  • Click "Evaluate" to generate a truth table for the painted diagram.
  • Press the Spacebar to toggle dark mode.
  • Toss a coin to your Witcher.
